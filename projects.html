Message app

I've created a real-time messaging app using Flutter with Firebase as the backend. Users can easily register and sign up to exchange messages seamlessly. The app offers a simple yet efficient platform for instant communication, designed to be intuitive and user-friendly.

Know Weather

A straightforward weather application providing users with real-time weather updates without location customization. Powered by the OpenWeather API, it offers simplicity by delivering essential weather information such as current conditions, temperature, wind speed, humidity, and precipitation forecasts. With its minimalist design, the app focuses on delivering quick and reliable weather data without the need for users to set specific locations.
