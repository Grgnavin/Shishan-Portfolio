Achievements

Git workshop - 2023

In front of a crowd exceeding 100 enthusiastic students, I spearheaded a highly successful workshop focused on Git and GitHub. Collaboratively organized by both myself and my dedicated team, this engaging event took place within the college campus and garnered widespread praise and participation.

2nd Runner Up Hult Prize OnCampus 2024

I led Team Eco Road to a remarkable achievement at the Hult Prize OnCampus event in 2024, securing the prestigious 2nd runner-up position. Our project, 'Revolutionizing Roads, Transforming Tires,' was a testament to our hard work and innovative spirit. Collaborating with the exceptional talents of Ms. Roshni Acharya (BBA) and Ms. Deepika Dangol (B.A. LLB), we showcased how diverse expertise can drive sustainability in infrastructure. Our focus on rubberized asphalt promises to reshape the future of roads. This accomplishment marks a significant milestone in our journey, and I am incredibly proud of our team's dedication and success.

Birthday Bank - 2022

As a Grade 12 student at Motherland Higher Secondary School, I was proud to be part of the transformative Birthday Bank initiative conducted by our students. We pooled our resources to support rural communities, funding projects like building classrooms and providing educational materials, showcasing the power of collective action and community service.
